How does it work?

Buy tickets with digital dollars. Then, wait the prize announcement to know if you are the lucky person who won all of the interest that accrued in the pool!. More details in our FAQ

1

Buy a ticket

Buy a PoolTogether ticket with DAI or USDC stablecoin. 1 DAI = 1 ticket. Buying more tickets and grouping them with other people will give you more chance of winning.

2

Sit back and relax

You will receive Pool DAI (plDAI), which represents your DAI holdings in PoolTogether. All the plDAI in the pool earns interest to fund the next prize.

3

Never lose

Wait until the next pool prize is awarded to know if you are the lucky person who won all of the interest that accrued in the pool. Whether you win or not, you get all of your money back! You can withdraw your savings and winnings at any time.
